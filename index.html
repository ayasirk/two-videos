Features
  • 1.
    How does Recrowdly Convert work?

    After signing up, embed the Recrowdly Convert Widget on your website in one easy step. Once the widget has been installed, your customers can leave testimonials, for you to manage, select and showcase from the dashboard.

  • 2.
    What kind of devices can I use?

    Testimonials can be recorded on iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows. The dashboard can also be accessed on all these devices.

  • 3.
    What browsers are supported?

    Recrowdly Convert is compatible with Safari and Google Chrome.

  • 4.
    What tools does Covete integrate with?

    Recrowdly Convert works effortlessly with all your existing tools and platforms such as Shopify, WordPress, Carrd, Squarespace, Framer, Webflow, Kajabi and much more.

  • 5.
    How is it different?

    Recrowdly Convert simplifies collecting, curating, and showcasing video testimonials through interactive widgets and content repurposing. This sets it apart as an effective tool for enhancing credibility, engagement, and conversions.

  • 6.
    What features does it have?

    Widget: Recrowdly Convert offers an interactive widget that seamlessly integrates into your website. This widget enables visitors to view and engage with authentic video testimonials, enhancing their trust in your brand.
    Wall of Reviews: With Recrowdly Convert, you can create an impressive "Wall of Love" by showcasing a collection of top-notch video testimonials. This dynamic presentation further reinforces your brand's credibility and encourages visitor engagement.
    Custom Email Invitations: Recrowdly Convert simplifies the process of gathering testimonials by allowing you to send customized email invitations to satisfied customers. This approach facilitates the collection of authentic and relevant video content.
    Dashboard Convenience: Recrowdly Convert's all-in-one dashboard centralizes the management of your testimonials. Easily curate, organize, and showcase the best testimonials, ensuring a seamless and efficient workflow.
    Live Analytics: Stay informed about the impact of your video testimonials with real-time analytics. Monitor engagement metrics, view rates, and other key performance indicators to continuously refine your strategy.

  • 7.
    How effective is it?

    Recrowdly Convert has proven highly effective in boosting credibility, trust, and conversion rates. Users who incorporate video testimonials report a significant increase in customer engagement and overall sales performance. The interactive nature of video testimonials captures attention and drives more meaningful connections, resulting in tangible business growth.

  • 8.
    How do I collect reviews?

    Recrowdly Convert lets you collect testimonials via custom emails for use in your campaigns and allows customers to leave video testimonials on your website's embedded widget. You can easily manage and display these testimonials from your dashboard.

  • 9.
    Can I upload my own reviews?

    Yes, you can upload your own reviews on the widget. You can be creative and use it to add any welcome messages for your customers.

  • 10.
    Does it support closed captions for users who have their sound turned off?

    Yes, closed caption is a feature available to all our users.

  • 11.
    Does it support internationalization / closed caption transcription / language for non-English speakers?

    We're actively developing this feature and it will be available soon.

  • 12.
    Can I customize the widget?

    The widget can be customized through the Recrowdly Convert dashboard to align with your brand’s identity.

  • 13.
    How is the widget installed?

    To integrate the widget into your webpage, simply insert the provided code just before the closing </head> tag of your HTML document.
    <script id="covete_script" value="brand_s78phynhsv" src="https://widget.Recrowdly Convert.com/index.js" defer ></script>

  • 14.
    How is the “Wall of Reviews” installed?

    To integrate the “Wall of Reviews”, simply copy the following code
    <div id="forSection" ></div>
    Paste the code where you want inside the <body> tag of the page.
    The location placed will determine where the section will appear.

  • 15.
    How easy is it to uninstall?

    Uninstalling is as easy as just removing the code in one step.
